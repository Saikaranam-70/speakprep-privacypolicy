SpeakPrep - Privacy Policy & Terms

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

SpeakPrep is a communication and speaking practice application designed to help users improve their speaking skills. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address (if login is used)
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings for speaking practice
  • Usage Data: App interactions, performance data
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide speaking practice features
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To analyze usage and fix bugs
  • To personalize user experience

4. Microphone Permission

SpeakPrep requires access to your device microphone to record your speech for practice and analysis. Audio recordings are used only for app functionality and are not shared without your consent.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • App performance monitoring

6. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Account Deletion

If you create an account, you have the right to delete your account and data.

You can request account deletion by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" option inside the app
  • Contacting us at: your-email@example.com

Once requested, your data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Data Safety

In accordance with Google Play policies, we declare:

  • We collect personal data such as email (if login is used)

  • Data is used only for app features and improvement
  • We do not sell user data

9. Permissions

We only request permissions necessary for app functionality.
