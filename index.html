SpeakPrep - Privacy Policy & Terms

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

SpeakPrep is a communication and speaking practice application designed to help users improve their speaking skills. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address (if login is used)
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings for speaking practice
  • Video Data: Video may be recorded, but only audio is extracted. Video is not stored.
  • Usage Data: App interactions and performance data
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide speaking practice features
  • To process and analyze audio recordings
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To fix bugs and enhance features

4. Microphone Permission

SpeakPrep requires microphone access to record your speech for practice and analysis. Audio data is used only for app functionality and is not sold or misused.

5. Audio & Video Usage

SpeakPrep may allow video recording for speaking practice. However, only audio is extracted for analysis. Video content is not stored, shared, or processed beyond extracting audio.

6. Notifications

SpeakPrep may send notifications such as reminders, updates, and practice suggestions. You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party services such as:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising services (e.g., Google AdMob)

8. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

9. Account Deletion

If you create an account, you have the right to delete your account and data.

  • Use the "Delete Account" option inside the app
  • Email us at: oneserve9@gmail.com

Your data will be deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Data Safety

  • We collect email (if login is used)
  • We collect audio data for app features
  • Video is not stored (only audio is extracted)
  • We do not sell user data

11. Permissions

  • Microphone: For recording speech
  • Camera: For optional video recording (audio extraction only)
  • Notifications: To send reminders and updates
  • Internet: For app functionality

We only request permissions required for app features.

12. Terms & Conditions

  • You must not misuse or disrupt the app
  • All content is protected by intellectual property laws
  • The app is provided "as is" without warranties

13. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
